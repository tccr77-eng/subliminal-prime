## Idea A — "Obsidian Signal"
**Design Movement:** Dark Brutalism meets Premium Tech
**Core Principles:**
- Jet-black canvas with electric blue as the single accent colour — no other colours
- Asymmetric layouts with large typographic anchors bleeding off-screen
- Deliberate tension between raw power and scientific precision
**Color Philosophy:** #0a0a0f background (near-black with a blue undertone), #3b82f6 electric blue accent, white text. The darkness signals seriousness; the electric blue signals energy and intelligence.
**Layout Paradigm:** Left-anchored hero text with full-bleed dark photography on the right. Product cards in asymmetric masonry grids. Sections separated by subtle gradient dividers rather than lines.
**Signature Elements:** Thin horizontal rule lines in blue, large oversized section numbers (01, 02, 03), subtle noise/grain texture on dark backgrounds
**Interaction Philosophy:** Hover states reveal blue glows; cards lift with a subtle shadow; CTAs pulse once on load
**Animation:** Fade-up entrance animations on scroll, staggered card reveals, smooth page transitions
**Typography System:** Syne (display, bold 700/800) + Inter (body, 400/500). Headlines are massive and left-aligned.

## Idea C — "Apex Dark" ← SELECTED
**Design Movement:** Premium Performance Brand (Nike/Apple meets neuroscience)
**Core Principles:**
- Absolute dark background with electric blue as the sole accent — zero compromise on contrast
- Bold, confident typography that commands attention without screaming
- Every element earns its place — ruthless whitespace discipline
- Scientific credibility expressed through clean structure, not decoration
**Color Philosophy:** #09090b background (zinc-950), #2563eb / #3b82f6 electric blue gradient accent, #ffffff white text, #a1a1aa zinc-400 for secondary text. The palette says: serious, premium, results-driven.
**Layout Paradigm:** Full-width dark sections with max-width content containers. Hero is left-aligned text + right-side dark photography. Product grid is 4-column on desktop. Sections alternate between dark and slightly-lighter-dark backgrounds for depth.
**Signature Elements:** Blue glowing underlines on key words, subtle radial gradient "glow" behind hero text, product cards with a dark glass-morphism effect
**Interaction Philosophy:** Every interactive element has a clear, satisfying response. Buttons glow on hover. Cards lift. The site feels alive but never gimmicky.
**Animation:** Framer Motion scroll-triggered fade-ups, staggered product card entrances, smooth route transitions
**Typography System:** Space Grotesk (display headlines, 700/800) + Inter (body, 400/500). Space Grotesk has a technical, engineered feel that matches the brand perfectly.
